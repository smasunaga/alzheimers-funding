How does the Alzheimer's Association stack up?

Federal Alzheimer's disease research funding is mostly centralized in the NIA, but other institutes under the umbrella of the NIH also contribute funds to new experiments. With the low and inconsistent levels of federal funding, nonprofit organizations like the Alzheimer's Association and venture philanthropy groups like the Alzheimer's Drug Discovery Foundation play increasingly important roles in supporting the work of researchers.
